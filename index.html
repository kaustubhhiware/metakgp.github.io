Metakgp

What is Metakgp?

Metakgp is a loose association of engineers, hackers, artists, and students from IIT Kharagpur, who collaborate on various technical and non-technical projects.

We celebrate freedom and openness; all our projects are free as in speech (and usually beer too).

Some of our projects

  • Metakgp Wiki: The community wiki for IIT Kharagpur, and our flagship project.

  • Question Paper Search: Instant search through previous years' question papers.

  • KGP TnP Noticeboard: A Google Group where notices put up on the T&P noticeboard are mirrored, along with attachments. Subscribe for regular updates.

  • The Kharagpur Documentation Project: A collection of interviews of the non-student residents of IIT KGP.
